About

We are team VirtualSense. Our goal is to organically provide ads to users watching Sports events, eSports, movies and TV series. We can seamlessly place ads as background elements within the video, for example banners or billboards, as if they were part of the scene.




Service

We provide AI solutions and services that help publishers, broadcasters, TV channels and advertisers increase their ads revenue all the way preserving uninterrupted engagement and viewership experience for audience.

Our technology expands the range of available advertising spaces and provides opportunity to maximize publisher's revenue potential.

Our services

  • Virtual overlays: physical signage replacement
  • Virtual inserts: create new ad placements inside video context
